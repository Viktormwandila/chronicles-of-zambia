Newspaper Overview

Welcome to The Chronicles of Zambia, a newspaper designed to provide insightful, data-driven journalism focused on empowering Zambian citizens with the knowledge they need to understand their country's role on the global stage.

Our mission is to bridge the gap in the current news landscape by offering deep analysis in various sectors including:

  • Economics
  • Health
  • Technology
  • Government processes
  • Youth & Education

Economics (Data-Driven Analysis)

Our economics section provides Zambians with deep, analytical insights on local and global economic trends, covering topics like:

  • GDP trends
  • Inflation
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Financial markets
  • Impact of economic policies on Zambians

Health (Data and Science-Based Insights)

The health section offers scientifically-backed information and data-driven reports on matters affecting Zambia and the wider world, including:

  • Health education
  • Disease prevention
  • Innovations in healthcare
  • Public health challenges
  • Sustainable healthcare systems in Zambia

Technology, Academics & Research

Our technology section explores advancements in tech and their implications for Zambia’s growth, including:

  • Digital technologies
  • Local research initiatives
  • Academic achievements
  • Research opportunities

Government & Civic Education

The government section is designed to educate Zambians about official processes, policies, and procedures. We cover:

  • Simplified civic information
  • Government policies
  • Citizen rights and responsibilities
  • Civic participation

Youth & Education

This section focuses on issues that matter to young Zambians, including:

  • Educational resources
  • Career guidance
  • Mental health awareness
  • Youth empowerment
  • Social issues affecting young people

Cultural Section

The cultural section celebrates Zambia’s rich cultural heritage and offers a look into:

  • Traditional Zambian sports and games
  • Unique physical, mental, and spiritual benefits of these games
  • Profiles of Zambian artists, musicians, and cultural leaders
  • Zambia's cultural heritage and traditions
  • How global culture influences Zambian traditions

Editorial Guidelines

Our editorial guidelines ensure that the content produced is:

  • Fact-based and well-researched
  • Neutral and free from political bias
  • Clear, concise, and accessible to a wide audience
  • Relevant to Zambian citizens and global topics

Code of Ethics for Writers

All writers must adhere to the following ethical standards:

  • Integrity in reporting facts
  • Avoiding conflicts of interest
  • Maintaining confidentiality where necessary
  • Providing proper attribution for sources
  • Respecting the dignity of all individuals

Submission & Citation Guidelines

All submissions must follow these guidelines:

  • Original, unpublished content
  • Proper citations for any data or sources used
  • Adherence to our format and length requirements
  • Submissions via approved channels

Privacy Policy

We take user privacy seriously and guarantee:

  • Protection of personal data
  • No sharing of data without consent
  • Secure data handling practices
  • Clear data retention policies

Terms & Conditions

By accessing and using our platform, users agree to:

  • Respect our intellectual property
  • Comply with our content usage policies
  • Avoid unlawful activities while on the platform

Writer's Agreement Contract

All writers are required to sign a contract agreeing to:
